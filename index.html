Biography

Massimo Daul was born in Milan, Italy, and has spent much of his life moving between the east coast of the United States and Europe. After attending UWC Adriatic for two years, he moved to New York City, where he has been studying Mathematics and Computer Science at NYU for the past three years.

His research focuses on low-resource machine learning, particularly in NLP and speech processing. Massimo is passionate about developing computational tools to help preserve endangered languages. His work spans speaker diarization, forced alignment, and optimization techniques.

In his free time, Massimo plays soccer, piano, and drums. He recently released an EP with the Third Street Band (Listen Here). He also loves to cook, bake, and spend time outdoors.
